What Affects Timing

The main things that affect the quote.

Amount of glazing

Window count, French doors, bifolds, skylights, and conservatory panels all add time.

Height and access

Two-storey and three-storey homes, awkward access, and delicate areas all affect the visit length.

Condition

A regularly kept property is quicker than a first clean or a job with heavy build-up.

Frequency

For a lot of homes, 6-weekly work is a good balance between keeping on top of things and keeping the cost sensible.

Why Keep It Regular

Regular rounds usually make life easier than one-off cleans.

Regular work usually keeps the windows looking better, avoids heavy build-up, and makes each visit a bit more predictable. That tends to work well whether the house is fairly simple or more involved.

Better finish

Less build-up usually means the property keeps a cleaner, more even look between visits.

More efficient visits

Regular work is easier to plan and price properly than irregular catch-up jobs.

Good for bigger homes too

If you like the place looking cared for, a steady maintenance round usually gives the best result.
